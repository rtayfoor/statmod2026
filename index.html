Details about ΣtatMod 2025 Conference

ΣtatMod2026 is organized under the aegis of UCLan Cyprus, with the support of the Cyprus Statistical Society and the Greek Statistical Institute and follows six successful and impactful conferences since 2020. T he general objective of ΣtatMod2026 conference is to bring together experts on statistical and probability modeling, considering both theoretical and applied points of view. It is also hoped that the presentations will shed light on the latest scientific advances in the field, discussing the major issues and the main future directions in this area.

Venue/Location

The conference will be hosted at the University of Central Lancashire Cyprus (UCLan Cyprus), located in Pyla, in the Larnaca district. Established in 2012, UCLan Cyprus is the first British university campus on the island, combining the academic standards of the UK higher-education system with a modern Mediterranean setting. The campus features contemporary lecture theatres, seminar rooms, a spacious auditorium, and dedicated networking areas, providing an ideal environment for academic exchange and collaboration. UCLan Cyprus is situated approximately 15 minutes from Larnaka International Airport and is easily accessible via major transport routes. The campus lies close to the coastline, offering visiting delegates a welcoming and pleasant environment with nearby accommodation options, cafés, and beach-side facilities. With a blend of academic excellence, modern infrastructure, and convenient travel connections, UCLan Cyprus offers an exceptional venue for our conference.
